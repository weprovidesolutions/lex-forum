About Us

Lex Forum Events and Venues offers professional event planning services which include event planning and management, event coordination, decoration, styling, invitations, souvenirs, food catering, and venue.

All throughout the years, Lex Forum has successfully organized numerous events and has turned thousands of dreams into reality. We always make sure that each of our clients is happy and satisfied with the outcome of their dream event. Lex Forum ensures that the clients are proactively involved from planning, budgeting to implementation and that they’re comments and suggestions are heard.

Lex Forum has a diverse team, each offering exceptional knowledge and contributions to ensure the success of every event that we handle. We are a team of talented and passionate individuals that work hand in hand to achieve our desired goals. Our events wouldn’t become successful without them.

Our business is making memories and we want to make it extraordinary.

Our Services

Venue

Lex Forum is one of the finest event centers in General Santos City and offers a spacious venue while still maintaining a classy and intimate setting.

Events

We arrange all sorts of events like weddings, birthdays, debuts, baptisms, anniversaries, seminars, conferences, and other social events.

Food Catering

We satisfy all our clients by providing the most appetizing menu for any occasion. From buffets to sit-down plated packages, we got it all covered.

Decorations

Lex Forum offers unique and trendy themes and styles for any occasion to help you achieve your dream event with a reasonable price.

Invitations

Lex Forum can provide you with high quality customized invitations that will best match your theme at a cheap price.

Souvenirs

Whatever the occasion is, we can offer unique gifts and souvenirs to your event guests that will surely make them remember your event fondly.
